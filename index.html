About Me

Hello! My name is Wesley Yates and I am currently enrolled as a student at UCF. My major is Ditigal media with my goal of being able to design my own games by the end of college. Ever since I was little, I have been interested in videogames, and my passion for them grew as I got older, now being interested in almost every aspect of them including gameplay, story, art, programing, character design, and music.
